## General Doors

| Key | Game |
|-----|------|
| S | Stack'em |
| M | RMastermind |
| N | NIALL |
| O | Oregon Trail |

**Stack'em** -- Tetris clone. Stack falling blocks and clear lines before the pile reaches the top. Single player, score-based.

**RMastermind** -- Win32 implementation of the classic Mastermind code-breaking game. Guess the hidden colour sequence within a limited number of attempts. Single player.

**NIALL** -- An AMOS-based chat bot door. Have a conversation with an AI character. Novelty/fun door, not a game as such.

**Oregon Trail** -- The classic 1970s educational game. Lead a wagon party from Independence, Missouri to Oregon's Willamette Valley. Manage food, ammunition, and supplies while surviving river crossings, illness, and bad weather.

## Adventure -- Muds & More

| Key | Game |
|-----|------|

**Ambroshia: Test of Time** -- Multi-player civilisation strategy game. Build your empire, manage resources, research technology, and compete against other players over daily turns.

**Barren Realms Elite (BRE)** -- Classic multi-player war strategy door. Manage land, population, and military forces. Attack rival players and defend your realm. Turn-based -- one set of actions per day.

**Death Masters** -- Multi-player combat door. Players fight monsters and each other to rise through the ranks. Daily turns, persistent world.

**DoorMud: The Land of the Forgotten** -- DOS-based multi-user dungeon. Explore a persistent text-adventure world, fight monsters, collect equipment, and advance your character through classic RPG mechanics.

**Drakeville** -- Fantasy RPG door set in a small village. Explore the surrounding lands, battle enemies, and work your way up to challenging the area's most dangerous foes.

**Usurper** -- Win32 dungeon-crawl RPG. Fight monsters in the dungeon, buy equipment in town, and work your way to the top of the player rankings. One of the most feature-rich classic door RPGs.

**Land of Devastation (LOD)** -- Post-apocalyptic multi-player strategy and RPG. Explore a ruined world, scavenge resources, build your character, and compete with other survivors for dominance.

**Vampires: Children of Darkness** -- Multi-player vampire-themed RPG. Hunt for blood, build your powers, and battle rival vampires in a dark gothic world.

## Legend Of the Red Dragon

| Key | Game |
|-----|------|
| 1 | LORD (New -- v4.07) |
| 2 | LORD (Old -- v4.06) |
| 3 | MZK LORD (New) |
| 4 | LORD II |
| S | MZK LORD (Special) |

**Legend Of the Red Dragon (LORD)** -- The definitive classic BBS door game. A fantasy RPG where you fight daily battles against forest monsters, build strength and gold, flirt with the inn keeper's daughter or Seth Able's son, and work towards challenging the Red Dragon. Two versions are available (v4.06 and v4.07) running separate game worlds.

**Legend Of the Red Dragon II** -- The official sequel to LORD. Expanded world with towns, guilds, and more complex quests. Same core daily-turn RPG structure as the original.

**MZK LORD** -- LORD running with the MZK Modification Kit (version 6). Adds significant gameplay enhancements, new events, extra commands, and expanded content on top of the standard LORD engine. Two MZK instances available (New and Special) with separate game worlds.

## Space Adventure

| Key | Game |
|-----|------|
| B | BBS Invaders! |
| P | Planets: TEOS |
| G | Galactic Warzone (New) |
| H | Galactic Warzone (Old) |
| S | Star Trek |

**BBS Invaders!** -- Space Invaders clone for the BBS. Shoot waves of descending aliens before they reach the bottom. Classic arcade action in ANSI.

**Planets: TEOS** -- Multi-player space empire strategy. Build planetary defences, develop your economy, research technology, and wage war across the galaxy. Turn-based with a persistent universe.

**Galactic Warzone** -- Multi-player space strategy and combat door. Claim star systems, build fleets, and fight for galactic control. Two separate game instances (New and Old) with independent universes.

**Star Trek** -- The classic BBS Star Trek game. Command the Enterprise through a grid of quadrants, hunt Klingon battle cruisers, dock at starbases to resupply, and protect the Federation before your energy runs out.

**Trade Wars: 2002** -- The legendary BBS space trading and combat game. Buy and sell commodities across hundreds of sectors, claim ports and planets, build a corporate empire, and fight (or ally with) other players. One of the deepest and longest-running BBS door games ever made.

## Hackers -- BBSCrash Menu

| Key | Game |
|-----|------|
| C | BBS Crash (New) |
| D | BBS Crash (Old) |

**BBS Crash** -- Hacker-themed door game. Use various techniques to infiltrate and crash rival BBS systems while defending your own. Two game instances (New and Old) with separate game states.

**Leech** -- BBS hacking simulation. Break into systems, download files, and avoid getting traced. Classic underground-themed door with a persistent world and player rankings.
